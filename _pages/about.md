Hi! I am Terezia Slaninakova, Ph.D. candidate (finishing in 2026) and researcher at Masaryk University, Brno, Czech Republic. 

I am a research engineer focused on building useful production-ready applications for processing of complex data.

I got my B.S.c-equivalent in Computer Science in Brno University of Technology in 2018, in my final thesis I focused on [generative AI applied to sound](https://www.vut.cz/www_base/zav_prace_soubor_verejne.php?file_id=181308) in [Speech@FIT lab](https://speech.fit.vut.cz). While studying I also worked at [Y Soft](ysoft.com) and [Adacta](https://adactainsurance.com) on creating automatic quality assurance pipelines.

For my M.S.c, I studied Artificial intelligence and Data processing, while working on projects at [CERIT-SC](https://www.cerit-sc.cz/), where I created ML pipelines for life tissue and remote sensing data. For my thesis, I [created an ML-based index high-dimensional vector data](https://is.muni.cz/th/f274m/).

I began my Ph.D. in Spring 2021, focused on extending my work on the ML-based vector index under the [GF23-07040K](https://starfos.tacr.cz/en/projekty/GF23-07040K#project-main) project co-lead with CAU in Kiel, Germany. The theoretical work ultimately lead to creating a similarity search system for finding proteins with similar structure in 200M+ proteins, [AlphaFind](https://alphafind.fi.muni.cz/), used by researchers worldwide. Recently, we extended its functionality with [AlphaFind v2](https://alphafind.ics.muni.cz/), indexing a total of 1B data. I'm finishing my Ph.D. in 2026 and exploring opportunities where I can apply my expertise in similarity search, ML systems, and production deployment.
